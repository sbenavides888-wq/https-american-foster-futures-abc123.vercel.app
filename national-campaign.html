National Movement for Foster Care Tuition Waivers

Every foster youth deserves tuition-free access to public colleges. We are building a nationwide movement to replicate Texas' model and establish cross-state reciprocity.

See Focus States View Campaign Map

The Goal

Texas offers one of the nation's strongest foster youth tuition waiver programs. Our movement works state-by-state to pass similar laws — and to honor waivers across state lines through reciprocity.
