Our Mission

Breaking the cycle of disadvantage for foster youth.

American Foster Futures empowers foster youth nationwide through policy advocacy and direct support for education, housing, and the transition out of care.

Read our full mission and story → · Our values →

The need is urgent.
Foster youth deserve the same opportunities as their peers.
See how we help
~23,000

Without support, many face homelessness, unemployment, and lost educational opportunities.

Stable housing and education are the most powerful protective factors.
